Thank you for your interest in opening a Sarovara depot. From this page you will receive the following information.
  • 1.
    What are the terms and conditions to open a Sarovara depot ?
      You will have to meet the following conditions to open a depot.
    • a)
      You are allowed only to sell drinking water that is processed, produced and purified according to standard provided by the Guidelines on Processed Drinking Water Production Standards 2017.
    • b)
      You are allowed only to sell drinking water from the companies that allow Sarovara to inspect their water producing and purifying facilities.
    • c)
      You are not allowed to sell at a higher price than what Sarovara has predetermined but you are allowed to sell at a lower price
    • d)
      In regular basis, you are required to bring water sample of drinking water that you are selling or want to sell from Sarovara depot to Sarovara for a regular test
    • e)
      You must address any advice, suggestion or complaint from customers immediately.
    • f)
      You must comply with any rules and regulations aimed to improve customer service and company’s growth
  • 2.
    What are the services you will receive from Sarovara after you open a depot ?
      Sarovara will provide the following services for the depot you open.
    • a)
      The amount of drinking water your depot needs from various companies will be delivered to your depot.
    • b)
      We will test the drinking water your depot sells for free.
    • c)
      We will promote and advertise your depot.
    • d)
      We will provide features your depot needs in our Sarovara app.
    • e)
      In Sarovara app, we will provide a platform to sell drinking water to your customer.
    • f)
      We will provide a service that allows customer to pay via Sarovara itself.
    • g)
      We will add additional feature according to depot’s demand.
    • h)
      Depots that sell at a lower price than Sarovara has determined will receive a discount that is proportionate to service charge.
  • 3.
    What is the service fee of Sarovara ?
    • a)
      If depot sells 250 or less water jars a day, the monthly charge would Rs.5000.
    • b)
      If a depot sells more than 251 or less than 500 jars a day, the charge would be Rs.1 per jar.
    • c)
      If a depot sells more than 501 jars a day, the charge would be Rs.2 per jar.
