Thank you for your interest in selling your company’s drinking water through Sarovara.
Any company that processes and purifies drinking water can sell its drinking water through Sarovara, if it meets Sarovara’s guidelines. Sarovara will test the quality of drinking water provided by any company that want to sell drinking water through Sarovara, it will routinely inspect water processing and purifying facilities and it will only sell and distribute drinking water that meet our quality standard. On daily basis, Sarovara will publish information about drinking water quality based on their companies and we will sell and distribute drinking water from companies that our customers choose.
In order to sell your company’s drinking water through Sarovara, please read thoroughly the following terms and conditions.
  • 1.
    I produce, process and purify drinking water in accordance with the standard set by the Government of Nepal and its subordinate offices (the Guidelines on Processed Drinking Water Production Standards 2017).
  • 2.
    I follow the sanitation guidelines set by the Government of Nepal and its subordinate offices (the Guidelines on Processed Drinking Water Production Standards 2017) while producing and processing, filling and cleaning water jars and also for factory sanitation.
  • 3.
    I will regularly pay taxes or any fees that is required by the Government of Nepal.
  • 4.
    I will allow Sarovara Drinking Water Pvt. Ltd. to inspect whether the drinking water my company produces, processes and purifies meet the standards set by (the Guidelines on Processed Drinking Water Production Standards 2017) and to publish such information through Sarovara app.
  • 5.
    I will allow Sarovara Drinking Water Pvt. Ltd. to make inspection about drinking water production, processing, purifying and sanitation work at any time.
  • 6.
    Once Sarovara Drinking Water Pvt. Ltd. examines the product my company produces, I will allow to put a Sarovara sticker to verify that it was tested and verified by this company.
  • 7.
    I will comply with the mentioned terms and conditions. I am aware that in case of non-compliance of the terms and conditions, Sarovara can ban my company from selling drinking water through Sarovara app.
