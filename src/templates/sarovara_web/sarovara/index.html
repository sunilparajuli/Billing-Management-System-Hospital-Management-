Quality Drinking Water at a Reasonable Price

Our company works to test whether the drinking water processed and purified by various companies meet the Guidelines on Processed Drinking Water Production Standards 2017, to publish the test results to the public for information, to routinely inspect water processing and purifying facilities to check their sanitation level, to provide a platform to buy the drinking water--that meet our quality standard--through our mobile app and deliver the purchased drinking water to homes and offices of our customers.

What we do?

Regular Quality Check

We do quality check of drinking water processed and purified by the companies that sell or want to sell drinking water through sarovara.

Regular Inspection

We routinely inspect water processing and purifying facilities of the companies that sell or want to sell drinking water through Sarovara.

Make Information Transparency

We regularly publish information about drinking water quality and sanitation information of the companies that sell or want to sell drinking water through Sarovara.

Buy Drinking Water

We will only buy drinking water from the companies that comply with the Guidelines on Processed Drinking Water Production Standards 2017.

Sales and Distributions

From our depots, we provide door-to-door delivery service of drinking water to our customers who purchase through our Sarovara app and other means.
